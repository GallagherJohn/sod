Popular Sod Varieties We Install

We work with high-quality, locally adapted sod types perfect for Northeast Florida climates.

St. Augustine (Floratam)

The most popular choice in Florida — thick, lush, and excellent shade tolerance.

Bermuda

Durable, fast-growing, and ideal for high-traffic areas with full sun.

Zoysia

Low-maintenance, dense turf with excellent drought and wear tolerance.

Bahiagrass

Tough, low-maintenance grass perfect for large areas and sandy soils.

Empire Zoysia

Soft texture, great shade tolerance, and minimal mowing required.

Palmetto St. Augustine

Improved shade and disease resistance with a beautiful dark green color.
